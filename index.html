TTSE GSS+ Bond Guidelines Integration

Use of Proceeds (UoP) Integrity IDB Invest Standard
Assurance Protocol: Green and Social bonds require ring-fenced capital. Funds must be tracked down to the project level (e.g., solar grid upgrades or low-income housing) to prevent capital leakage.
System Logic: The telemetry engine maps capital outflows directly against approved TTSE taxonomies, providing investors with auditable, real-time Use of Proceeds reporting.
Sustainability-Linked Bonds (SLBs) & Financial Penalties KPI Trigger
Assurance Protocol: Unlike UoP bonds, SLBs are tied to corporate-level targets (e.g., reducing operational emissions by 30%). Failing to hit the telemetry target triggers a coupon "step-up," costing the issuer millions in increased interest.
System Logic: The dashboard continuously audits SLB KPI performance, providing predictive warnings if the issuer is at risk of triggering a financial penalty.
